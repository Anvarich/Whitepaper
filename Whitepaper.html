TURAN COIN Whitepaper

Version 1.0 – July 2025

Introduction

TURAN COIN is a decentralized BEP-20 token created to unite, empower, and represent the shared cultural, historical, and economic heritage of Turkic nations. By enabling secure, transparent transactions and providing a foundation for future Web3 projects, TURAN COIN aims to become a cornerstone of digital sovereignty in the Turkic world.

Purpose

The project seeks to build a unified ecosystem where communities from Turkic nations can engage in blockchain-based finance, NFT art, historical preservation, and more.

Tokenomics

  • Total Supply: 1,000,000 TURAN
  • Token Standard: BEP-20 (Binance Smart Chain)
  • Decimals: 18
  • Contract Address: 0x4e492ab62aE51ebD421063aDE5B6ABD2c34F23Bc
  • Initial Distribution: 100% owned by the project wallet, with a roadmap for decentralized allocation.

Utilities

  • Peer-to-peer payments
  • DAO governance (future)
  • Exclusive access to TURAN NFT collections
  • Cultural event access and rewards

Conclusion

TURAN COIN is more than a token — it’s a movement to digitize and preserve the legacy of Turkic civilization in the new Web3 era.
